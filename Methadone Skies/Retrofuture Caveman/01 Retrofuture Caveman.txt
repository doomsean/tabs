==== Notes ====

There are two guitars here. Normally I'd assign the left channel to Guitar I and the right to
Guitar II, but, in this case, the significantly more prominent part is on the right (not far from
center), so I've assigned it to guitar I.

The first fiew minutes of the track are in C major and it sounds very jubilant (very post-rock).
It switches to a minor tonality at around the 4-minute mark, though, and stays that way until the
end, occasionally borrowing the major third again, however.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xWw_n0YrX-Q
