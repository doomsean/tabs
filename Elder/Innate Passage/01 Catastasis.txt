NOTE: There are several ways you could play this, and it's hard to say exactly what Nick is doing
without any live video footage, so I may be wrong in certain parts on which notes are picked and
which are hammer-ons, pull-offs, or even slides. This sounds very close to me, though.

Also, lift your ring finger when you're not picking the 6th string, but be sure to mute the string
somewhat so that the low C doesn't ring out as a pull-off. I do it by very lightly touching the
bottom string with the wrist edge of my palm near the bridge. That way, I don't have to do it with
my left thumb, which, rather than wrapping over the top of the fretboard, I have planted on the
back of the neck (for the first three lines, anyway; much easier to play).

Finally, I play almost all of the picked notes here with downstrokes. Do what feels most
comfortable to you, of course!
