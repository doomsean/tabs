==== Notes ====

Currently, as of January 2023, there exists no live footage of this song. Ergo, I can't be 100%
certain about every part. I think it's very close, however, and the guitars aren't terribly
difficult to hear (unlike in Reflections of a Floating World, for example).

Guitar I is mixed to the left and Guitar II is on the right (for the most part).

For now, I think I'd say that the song is in G, and that it modulates between major and minor
(Mixolydian and Dorian, to be more specific). Having said that, the tonal center seems to shift
quite a bit, and it modulates both in the 3rd _and_ the 7th. Very interesting...
