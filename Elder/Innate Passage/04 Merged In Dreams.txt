==== Notes ====

Currently, as of December 2022, there exists no live footage of this song. Ergo, I can't be 100%
certain about every part. I think it's very close, however, and the guitars aren't terribly
difficult to hear (unlike in Reflections of a Floating World, for example).

Guitar I is mixed to the left and Guitar II is on the right. Guitar III is generally near the
center of the mix.

Also note that certain parts of the song aren't transcribed at ~122 bpm (because it's easier to
notate at a different tempo, but it should be fairly obvious where this happens).

I'm also not sure yet if I'd say the key/scale was Dm or G Dorian, though I'm leaning toward the
latter. It seems a bit more rooted in G at times (same set of notes, obviously).
