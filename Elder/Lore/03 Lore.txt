==== Notes ====

Unless otherwise noted, I would describe the tone of all guitar parts on this track as a heavy,
saturated crunch. Reverb and delay are used often as well, plus the occasionaly phaser and wah.
Refer to the accompanying notes file for additional information.

adhere to Nick DiSalvo's live performance (and what seems easiest to me).
