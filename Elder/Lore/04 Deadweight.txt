==== Notes ====

Unlike the rest of the tracks on Lore, this one is in D standard tuning. It's also comparatively
simple. Taken together, this leads me to believe that Deadweight may have been written earlier,
perhaps around the same time as Dead Roots Stirring or the Spires Burn / Release EP. Obviously I
can't say for certain.

The various guitar tones and effects aren't terribly difficult to figure out. It sounds like the
same base crunch tone from the rest of the album, with occasional use of fuzz and manipulation of
the volumne knob on the guitar. Delay and reverb are used heavily throughout. There's also a lot of
wah in this track. Refer to the accompanying notes file for additional information.

Finally, upstrokes and downstrokes are noted in places where it may not be obvious. These generally
adhere to Nick DiSalvo's live performance (and what seems easiest to me).
