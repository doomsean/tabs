==== Notes ====

Unless otherwise noted, this is transcribed at 1.5x time (117 bpm) to better accommodate the
pervasive triplet feel and accurately portary note distance, which is difficult to do in ASCII
format. In other words, for this tab, four dashes represent one beat in the song, rather than the
standard three dashes. And what would normally be denoted as triplets are denoted here as eighth
notes (one dash).

Tonally speaking, this track sounds like it was recorded with Nick DiSalvo's Gibson SG into a
Hiwatt circuit; likely the Sound City 120 that was modded to DR103 spec. There's a heavy crunch
tone throughout, maybe with a bit of fuzz from a BAT Pharaoh (diodes lifted?). And certainly
there's a lot of reverb and delay in many parts as well. And wah... lots of wah, especially in
the solos, and especially when played live.

Finally, upstrokes and downstrokes are noted in places where it may not be obvious. These generally
adhere to NDS's live performance (and what seems easiest to me).

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CAde5EgpWY4
