==== Notes ====

As with Release, this track sounds like it was recorded with Nick DiSalvo's Gibson SG into a
Hiwatt circuit; likely the Sound City 120 that was modded to DR103 spec. There's a heavy fuzz tone
throughout, which is almost certainly a BAT Pharaoh (diodes lifted?). The second solo and various
background layers feature heavy wah and phaser use. And certainly there's a lot of reverb and delay
as well, as with most Elder songs.

Upstrokes and downstrokes are noted in a few places where it may not be obvious. These generally
adhere to NDS's live performance (and what seems easiest to me).
