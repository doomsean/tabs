==== Notes ====

DRS is particularly interesting because it's one of the few major-ish Elder tracks (Mixolydian is
considered "major" and only differentiates from major/Ionian in that it has a flat 7th).

Tonally, it sounds very simple. I'd be shocked if it was much more complicated than Nick's '61 SG,
some kind of fuzz (or a RAT), a delay pedal (EQD Dispatch Master?), and his Sound City 120 (modded
to DR103 spec). I can get a very similar tone with my own SG, Pharaoh, El Capistan, and Hi-Tone
amp, which is also adapted from the Hiwatt DR circuit.

Live video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FHpvUepk0Rk
