==== Notes ====

This track is fairly straightforward compared to the others on DRS, and probably the easiest to
play; a great intro to Elder for any guitarist who might be new to the band's catalog. It's mostly
just simple riffs and pentatonic shredding (very tastefully done).

Tonally, it's very similar to the rest of DRS; Nick's SG plus a few pedals, into his modded Sound
City 120 (DR103 spec). When the guitar is double-tracked, or even when the two guitars are playing
separate parts, I believe that one is often set to the "middle" pickup position (neck + bridge) and
the other is set to the bridge alone. It could also be just the neck pickup alone, but it sounds a
bit more (to me) like a combination of the two.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KUNNzioJYNM
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yeMVgQFrM2I
