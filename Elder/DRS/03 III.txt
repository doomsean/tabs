Obviously I haven't transcribed the acoustic guitar parts. I don't have one, and it doesn't
interest me all that much besides. Sorry! In the videos linked below, Nick plays an alternate part
in lieu of the acoustic. It doesn't seem terribly difficult to figure out, and I leave that as an
exercise for the intrepid reader. :-)

a bit less confident in the (precise) accuracy of this transcrition vs. others that I've done.
There was definitely some guesswork involved, which I've noted along the way.
