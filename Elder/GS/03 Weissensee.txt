==== Notes ====

I've only trasncribed the outro for this track because it seems obviously preconceived and more
structured than the preceding 16 minutes.

Guitar I and II generally seem to be double-tracking (with perhaps minor variances here and there),
and Guitar III is shredding in C minor for basically the entire two-and-a-half minutes, which I
also haven't transcribed. It's a great way to practice solo improvisation. Stick to C minor
pentatonic at first, then add the two and the flat six... or even the six (a Dorian tone), which
can be heard at various points.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qM79S2LiYnQ
