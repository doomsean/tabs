==== Notes ====

As with all of the tracks on Reflections (and Lore), this sounds like a very loud Marshall Plexi to
me. Obviously there's heavy fuzz and various other effects in certain parts, but the core "crunch"
tone sounds like a Plexi. And Nick is almost certainly using his '61 Reissue SG for the entire
album.

Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aL69-U9cngo
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=13QcPx04AeQ

a third guitar playing the melody, and then a fourth guitar playing a scale-tone harmony below the
melody. Live, Michael starts with the melody (Guitar II) and then switches to harmony, while Nick
starts with power chords and switches to the melody... which is really cool. What I've transcribed
here is the isolated power chords (Guitar I) and the melody. I leave the harmony as an exercise
for the reader. You can see Michael playing part of it in one of the videos linked above.
