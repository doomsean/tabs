==== Notes ====

This is one of my favorite tracks on Reflections. Admittedly, and somewhat frustratingly, I don't
really know what key it's in. Sometimes it feels like C minor (natural/Aeolian), sometimes it feels
like D minor, and other times it feels like F Dorian. C minor and F Dorian obviously share the same
notes chords, so... well, anyway, I don't know. The tonal center seems to move around quite a lot.

As with all of the tracks on Reflections (and Lore), this sounds like a very loud Marshall Plexi to
me. Obviously there's heavy fuzz and various other effects in certain parts, but the core "crunch"
tone sounds like a Plexi. And Nick is almost certainly using his '61 Reissue SG for the entire
album.

Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.
