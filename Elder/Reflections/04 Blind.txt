==== Notes ====

As with all of the tracks on Lore, this sounds like a very loud Marshall Plexi to me. Obviously
there's heavy fuzz and various other effects in certain parts, but the core "crunch" tone sounds
like a Plexi. And Nick is almost certainly using his '61 Reissue SG for the entire album.

Guitar I is generally mixed left and Guitar II is mixed right, although sometimes one or both
is/are in the center. The other guitar parts are usually in the center.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-ZQoelsbUjc
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aL69-U9cngo
