==== Notes ====

As with all of the tracks on Reflections (and Lore), this sounds like a very loud Marshall Plexi to
me. And Nick is almost certainly using his '61 Reissue SG for the entire album. Heavy reverb and
delay in various parts likely courtesy of an Earthquaker Devices Dispatch Master.

Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IS8JOlNDD3s
