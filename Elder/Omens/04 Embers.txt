==== Notes ====

As of right now (January 2021), there exists no live video footage of the band playing any of the
tracks from Omens. Because of this, I can't be sure that what I've transcribed below is entirely
accurate. I believe the *notes* to be mostly correct (except for the few parts that are difficult
to hear, which I've highlighted), but I don't know that Nick and Michael are playing their
respective parts the same way (in the same part of the neck, for example). Ergo, when video does
begin to surface, there may be slight revisions made to the transcription.

Tonally speaking, Nick and Michael recently did a "Rig Rundown" with Premier Guitar in which they
describe the guitars, amps, and pedals used to record the album...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5uFAFBF59vg

Generally speaking, Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.
Occasionally, however, one or the other is positioned more to the center.
