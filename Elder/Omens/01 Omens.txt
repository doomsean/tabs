==== Notes ====

As of right now (January 2022), there exists no live video footage of the band playing this track.
Ergo, I can't be sure that what I've transcribed below is entirely accurate. I believe the *notes*
to be mostly correct (except for the few parts that are difficult to hear, which I've highlighted),
but I don't know that Nick and Michael are playing their respective parts the same way (in the same
part of the neck, for example). Ergo, when video does begin to surface, there may be slight
revisions made to the transcription.

Regarding keys, the song starts in E Dorian and switches to C Dorian at around the seven minute
mark. It's a cool transition.

Regarding the guitar tones, Nick and Michael recently did a "Rig Rundown" with Premier Guitar in
which they described the guitars, amps, and pedals used to record the album...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5uFAFBF59vg

Generally speaking, Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.
Occasionally, however, one or the other is positioned more to the center.
