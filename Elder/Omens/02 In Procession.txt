==== Notes ====

This is one of my favorite tracks from Omens. Note that the timing is a bit weird in that the
tempo (kept by the drums) is 132 bpm, but it's much easier to transcribe and to "think" about the
guitars at 198 bpm (1.5 x 132). Only in one or two places, which I've noted, have I transcribed
them at 132. Everywhere else, it's 198. And while it's easier to count the guitars at 198, I still
set my metronome to 132 when playing along or practicing a part.

Tonally speaking, Nick and Michael recently did a "Rig Rundown" with Premier Guitar in which they
describe the guitars, amps, and pedals used to record the album...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5uFAFBF59vg

Also, for the most part, Guitar I is on the left (in the mix) and Guitar II is on the right.
Occasionally, however, one or the other is positioned more to the center.

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TCzLI7_O-rk
