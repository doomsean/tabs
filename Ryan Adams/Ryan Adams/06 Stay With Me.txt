==== Notes ====

Reminder: all fret numbers are relative to the capo.

Guitar I is either in the center or on the left (in the mix), and Guitar II, when present, is on
the right. In this particular song, Guitar I feels very slightly right-of-center when it's the
only guitar track (during the verse, for example).

Video footage used for corroboration/reference...

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kSht9HuGAW4
