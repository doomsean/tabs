==== Notes ====

The acoustic track plays a fairly minor role in this song and isn't played live with the band, so I
haven't transcribed it. The chord progression, however, is obviously the same as what the electric
guitars are playing. You could also just play everything here on an acoustic. :-)

Guitar I is mixed to the left and Guitar II is on the right.

Reminder: all fret numbers relative to the capo.
