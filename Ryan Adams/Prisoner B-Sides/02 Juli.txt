==== Notes ====

I would say that this song modulates in key between Am and C. Obviously those are relative keys and
they share the same set of notes, but in some parts the Am feels like the root and in other parts
the C feels like the root.

Guitar I is playing the rhythm (chords) in the left channel and Guitar II is playing the melody
lines and the arpeggios in the right channel. Both sound Fender-ish to me, with Guitar II in
particular sounding very much like a Jazzmaster (or possibly a Strat).

Reminder: all fret numbers relative to the capo.
