==== Notes ====

There's an awful lot going on in the various riffs throughout the entire song; ghost notes, muted
mutes, audible string pull-offs that happen simultaneously with picked notes, etc... I've tried to
annotate in-place where possible, but sometimes there isn't enough room. Look for the NOTEs in each
section for additional info. And bear in mind that Mark often doesn't play (seemingly) identitical
riffs in same way twice. There are often subtle variations, all of which I've tried to capture.

Complexity and improvisation aside, the song is basically a vamp on Dm > C, and I don't think he
ever hits a note outside the C major scale.

Also note that Mark is very disciplined with strict alt-picking (up, down, up, down...) on eighth
notes. I've annotated with "u" and "d" where it seems like it my be helpful. When not annotated,
and when in doubt, it's very likely alt-picking. Obviously this doesn't apply to songs/parts
wherein he uses hybrid- or finger-picking.

Finally, his playing is very percussive, and many of the single note lines throughout the song
(which is most of the song) are lightly to moderately muted.
